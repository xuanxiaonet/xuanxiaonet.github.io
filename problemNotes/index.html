各浏览器navigator.userAgent对象判断

判断手机：navigator.userAgent.match(/(phone|pad|pod|iPhone|iPod|ios|iPad|Android|Mobile|BlackBerry|IEMobile|MQQBrowser|JUC|Fennec|wOSBrowser|BrowserNG|WebOS|Symbian|Windows Phone)/i)

var ua = navigator.userAgent.toLowerCase();

判断微信:var isWeixin = ua.indexOf('micromessenger') != -1;

判断微博:var isWeiBo = ua.indexOf("weibo") != -1;

判断今日头条:var isTouTiao = ua.indexOf("newsarticle") != -1;
